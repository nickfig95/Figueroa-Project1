the art of worlds

this site is dedicated to the beautiful art of porter robinson's album and live show, worlds.

porter robinson released worlds on august 12, 2014

porter used his passion for videogames, manga, and japanese culture to create a vision for his world

the art style was indluenced by surrealist and escapist art

along with the release of the album, porter announced a 40 date tour of north america

the artwork was developed with the artists at invisible light network

porter brought his vision to life with the creation of his live show

porter uses the artwork to tell a story about escapism, fantasy, and destiny

the artwork can also be seen in the visuals for his live show

porter continues to evolve and update his world as he releases new music and goes on tour
